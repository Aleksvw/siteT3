About Knowles Liquor

Our Story

Knowles Liquor was founded in 1985 by Mr. Samuel Knowles, a passionate connoisseur of fine spirits with a vision to bring the best selection of wines, liquors, and beers to Nassau. What started as a small retail shop has grown into one of the premier liquor destinations in the Bahamas, serving both retail customers and wholesale clients.

Mr. Knowles personally selects every product in our store, traveling to vineyards, distilleries, and breweries around the world to ensure we offer only the highest quality products to our customers.

Today, Knowles Liquor remains a family-owned business, with Mr. Knowles' daughter, Sarah, now managing the day-to-day operations while maintaining the same commitment to quality and customer service that has been our hallmark for nearly four decades.
